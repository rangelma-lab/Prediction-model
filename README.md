A predictive model was developed to forecast future sales of Vanish brand cleaning products. Time series analysis techniques
were used with an ARIMA model.
An ARIMA time series model was applied to the data segmented by region, and the cases of Region 1 and Region 2 were analyzed.
• The optimal parameters were found to be p=8, d=2, and q=0 for both Region 1 and Region 2.
• The RMSE was 1831.55 and the MAPE was 9.12 for Region 2, and the RMSE was 888.94 and the MAPE was 8.09 for Region 1.
• A general upward trend in earnings is projected for Regions 1 and 2 over the next two months. However, this growth will not
be linear or constant week to week, as it will be influenced by sales variations, with some weeks showing higher performance 
and others showing lower activity.
